About Us

Vaccines save millions of lives every year. Developing a safe and effective COVID-19 vaccine is a big step forward in our global effort to end the pandemic and get us back to doing better with the people we love.

We always try and help the Vietnamese people to fight the covid-19 pandemic. The goal of the project is that we always focus on giving our best support to our compatriots and try to keep people living in Vietnam safe and including foreigners.
